OUR STORY

Built by a Student, for Students

MK

Hi, I'm Maira Khan.

As a Class 12 student preparing for college admissions, I experienced firsthand how overwhelming the process can be. Every college has different eligibility criteria, deadlines, scholarships, entrance exams and application procedures.

Students often spend weeks jumping between websites, YouTube videos and PDFs just to make one important decision.

That's why I started building MeritMatch—a single platform designed to simplify the entire college admission journey.

Why MeritMatch?

Choosing a college shouldn't feel like solving a puzzle. MeritMatch combines trusted information, AI-powered recommendations and personalized guidance to help students confidently choose the right college, discover scholarships and never miss an important deadline.
